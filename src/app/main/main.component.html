Books

Words are her passion. Her creation is the emotion of her subconscious mind. From her childhood, She started her journey with poetry, she loved to play with words.When she grew up,the words of her heart found a place inside two covers. She realized that a book doesn't carry not only words of mind but also gives a lifelong identity of a person. A writer wants to live through her own creation. In the same way she wants to live in this earth through her beautiful creation. She is the one who knows how to fly like a bird in the sky, knows how to float like a cloud in all inaccessible ways, knows how to give light to this earth like morning bright sun. Who can stop such an artist?

She has become an art in her own right. She is the inspiration of girls and power of women. She didn't learn to lose. She likes to take difficult things as a challenge. Poetry has penetrated the pores of the every cell of her body.
Many famous person said that Mili Das is a name among the born poets.

Award & Recognition

She has received many accolades for her poetry at West bengal and abroad. Overcoming all adversities, today she has crossed the borders of the country and started her journey to abroad.
